# Union Budget 2026-27 Analysis

**Objective:** To analyze the 2026-27 Indian Union Budget allocations, identifying top-funded ministries, high-growth schemes, and core fiscal priorities.

**Data:** Union Budget 2026-27 (Raw and cleaned CSVs provided).

**Methodology:** Engineered a structured database and executed SQL queries utilizing aggregations and window functions to extract year-over-year fiscal trends.

**Outputs:** Raw query results exported as CSVs for transparency.

**Key Findings:**
* **Top Ministries:** The Department of Rural Development received the highest allocation (₹2,28,480 Cr), followed by Drinking Water and Sanitation (₹74,862 Cr) and School Education (₹63,010 Cr).
* **Flagship Schemes:** Viksit Bharat-GRAM (₹95,692 Cr) and Jal Jeevan Mission (₹67,670 Cr) dominate scheme-wise funding, indicating a strong policy focus on rural livelihoods and infrastructure.
* **Growth Priorities:** The Irrigation Census and Flood Management (FMBAP) schemes saw the highest Year-over-Year growth in allocations (100% and 77.1%, respectively).
